** Hospital Management **

A Hospital Management Project involves creating a comprehensive system to streamline various operations within a healthcare facility.

It typically includes modules for patient registration, appointment scheduling, electronic health records (EHR), billing, pharmacy management, and staff administration.

The goal is to enhance efficiency, improve patient care, and provide a centralized platform for managing the diverse aspects of hospital operations.
